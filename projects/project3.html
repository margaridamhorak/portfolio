VOGUE X Reve

Graphic and Product Design | Group Project

This project was a collaboration with master's degree students and VOGUE Portugal. We had to develop a product package featuring a shower gel and additional items designed to appeal to the younger generation, particularly Gen Z. Our masters agency, Chinfrim, specifically requested that the shower gel and packaging should be inspired by the theme of surrealism.
