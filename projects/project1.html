A minha Pegada Ecologica

Architecture.

Individual Project.

In order for me to finish high school I had to make a final project called PAP. My project “A minha Pegada Ecológica”, is centered around transforming an existing house into an eco-friendly dwelling, emphasizing the harmonious integration of interior and exterior spaces. This will be accomplished through bioclimatic architecture, aligning with three of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals: good health and well-being, clean water and sanitation, and affordable and clean energy.
